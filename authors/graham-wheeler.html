Bio

Graham is a software engineer working at Google on Dart testing infrastructure. Before Google he spent a decade at Microsoft on everything from mobile search to smart watches to peer-to-peer network cryptographic protocols, was chief architect at an Internet firewall company, and spent a decade in academia. He lives in Redmond with his wife and two daughters, where he enjoys music, cycling, skiing, cooking, hiking, and mathematics, in roughly that order.
